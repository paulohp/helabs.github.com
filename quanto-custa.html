QUANTO CUSTA MEU SOFTWARE?

  • One of our big specialties is integrating systems. When ESPN Brazil, a giant in sports journalism, approached us to build their first mobile portal, it was a a huge chance to write some history. We ended up developing and integrating an entirely new mobile application within their existing CMS system in record time.

  • Already on the first week's iteration, we created a functional version of the site and developed a web service to exchange information, like data and files, between the different systems.

  • After 3 weeks of work, there were two complete solutions deployed to production supporting millions of visitors simultaneously, with no client IT involvement.

  • The web service also had a great benefit: it ended up turning into a commercial solution for distributing content to other devices, like smart-TVs.

  • We had also considered adding responsive design to automatically adjust the different media formats like text, audio, image or video.

  • So what's the net gain for contracting an agile team?

    For this project, continuous improvement and self-organization between the team helped optimize time and anticipate product vision and features. For example: on the next iteration, we already had planned to support Firefox OS, and responsive design.
